The source of truth never moves. The archive is always plain files; the HNSW layer is always an accelerator that you can rebuild from scratch any night and never need to migrate. That property is the entire reason this design survives a five-year horizon — your durability doesn't depend on Anthropic, on Ruflo, on any vendor still being around. It depends on cp and grep, both of which will outlive all of us.
